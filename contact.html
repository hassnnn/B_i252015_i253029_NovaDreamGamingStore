Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on your location. You can calculate shipping costs during checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused products in their original packaging. If you receive a defective product, we'll cover return shipping costs.

How does the Smart Compatibility Checker work?

Our compatibility checker analyzes the specifications of components you select (CPU, motherboard, GPU, RAM, PSU) to ensure they work together. It checks socket types, power requirements, physical dimensions, and other technical specifications.

Do you offer technical support for PC building?

Yes, our team of experts is available to help with any questions you have during your build process. We offer free guidance via email, chat, or phone for customers who purchase components from us.
